Instructions

Welcome to the fish tank! Here you can find all sorts of fishes. Click the tab labelled "Fish" and drag some aquatic critters into the tank. You can also check out the "Settings" tab and change the refresh rate of the tank, add some fun multipliers, and export/import your fish tank. Thanks a ton for playing!
